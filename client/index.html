Hackbit

The Hackbit habittracker lets you create both bad and good habits, keeps track of how you are doing and it is even possible to send you daily notifications.

Millions of people are using Hackbit daily for perfecting their day, so what are you waiting for?

Features

Easy to use

Hackbit has a clear design with built in tips and guidelines for getting you on your way as fast as possible.

Customizable

Hackbit offers a customizable home screen where you can customize evrey aspect of the habit you could possibly think of.

Free

Hackbit is completely free of charge, which is probably it's most liked feature.
